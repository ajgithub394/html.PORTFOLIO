MY MOST FAVOURITE MOVIE WATCHED RECENTLY

THE DARK KNIGHT TRILOGY

1. BATMAN BEGINS

SUMMARY

This movie was released in 2005. It tells us the story of Bruce
Wayne, son of a billionare family. He took martial arts training
and returned to his city, Gotham(fictional) to avenge the killing of
his parents and decides to fight for justice.

2. THE DARK KNIGHT

SUMMARY

In this movie Batman(Bruce Wayne) fights a new criminal in Gotham
city , named as the Joker along with the police commisioner and
the district attorney.

3. THE DARK KNIGHT RISES

SUMMARY

In this movie Batman fights a mercenary named Bane
